Projects

Datastore
  • A ticket would be generated if any datacenter host had hardware issues, or was out of contact for 15 minutes.
  • Any small config change mistake, could open thousands of tickets since those hosts were unavailable.
  • Closed tickets if the host was up, thereby reducing manual effort in ticket closure.
  • Generated pending ticket reports, providing accurate information on which tickets the team needs to work on.
  • Leveraged redis to maintain and close queue consisting of thousands of background jobs.
Almanac
  • Replaced earlier process of shift creation; in which keeping track of how many shifts a person did was a hassle.
  • Provided updated list of associates in team which would be updated everyday.
  • Tracks leaves, comp-offs and graveyard shifts.
  • Decreased time to prepare monthly shift plan to 1 hour from 5 hours.
  • Removed the issue of sharing the plan, across shifts.
Effort Tracker
  • Helps track effort for each associate, segregated by issue also while tracking effort spent and time taken.
  • Provides crucial information in the form of a dashboard regarding time spent on each activity category.
Paging Dashboard
  • The offshore team, earlier had to determine who was in shift and then find their contact details in the process of contacting them.
  • This application leveraged PagerDuty API, made contacting the onshore team simple.
